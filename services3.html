POLITICAL PARTY FINANCING

100% Citizens | Engaze Mobilize Sanze

Description

At its press conference on December 8, 2018, the Komite 13 Oktob ('K13O') introduced the Citizen Primary - an open, democratic and transparent model where citizens could choose their candidates. Citizens had the opportunity to send suggestions / proposals to improve the process until 26 December. The K13O had received several proposals that helped finalize the model.

The K13O launched the Citizen Primary officially on January 10, 2019 and more than 76 citizen candidates submitted their applications through its website www.ensamcitoyens.com. Subsequently, a committee of wise men, with different non-political profiles, validated the applications.

More than 30 working meetings have been completed since the launch of the citizen primary. At the national convention with the candidates registered on March 30, 2019, the candidate-citizens elected the executive bureau, validated the name of the party and the party structure.

This is a first in the political history of the country where a political formation was created by a process of participative democracy, resulting from a citizens' initiative.

A new political party was founded bearing the name '100% Citizens' .

Party values ​​are based on three main pillars, integrity , proactivity and synergy . It is a party that has been created by citizens and for citizens.
