PROMOTION OF LOCAL ARTISTS

Description

This project, promoted by Smartcitizen, aims to sustain support for artists showcasing the talents of young people who are unemployed and / or from modest or difficult backgrounds. After supporting a group of young artists in making a compilation record under the name 'Revelasion 2k17' led by Joyce Veerasamy. Real artistic talents were discovered among these young people, it is now a question of allowing them to acquire the necessary music material to the promotion of their works by organizing concerts across the island whereas today the rental of these materials is a hindrance to the development of their art.


Objectives

The objectives of this capacity building are to pool the resources and skills of these artists in order to:

  • To make known their talents
  • Create synergy by pooling resources
  • Improve their creativity through exchange and meetings with the public and other professionals in the sector
